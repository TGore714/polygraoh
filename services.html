What is Polygraph?

Psycho-physiological Detection of Deception (PPD) uses instruments to record involuntary physiological responses—like respiration, cardiovascular activity, and electrodermal activity—while the subject is presented with specific stimuli.

  • "Psycho": Mental/emotional response
  • "Physiological": Bodily changes
  • "Detection of Deception": Interpreted by a trained examiner

Used in criminal investigations, intelligence work, security screenings, and civil truth verification.

1. Criminal Testing

  • Law enforcement investigations
  • Prosecutorial or post-conviction reviews
  • Casino and tribal regulatory compliance

2. Pre-Employment Testing

For local, state, and federal agencies hiring for sensitive or critical roles.

3. Domestic & Infidelity Testing

  • Clarifies infidelity concerns
  • Supports emotionally charged decisions
  • Encourages honest dialogue for rebuilding trust

4. Defense Attorney Testing

  • Assess client credibility
  • Prepare for trial or plea
  • Support motions for dismissal or reductions

5. Drug Court Testing

  • Detect compliance or drug use
  • Support enforcement/treatment decisions
  • Reinforce accountability in rehab programs
