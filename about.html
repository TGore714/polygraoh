About Our Examiner

Verity Polygraph Services, LLC is led by a polygraph examiner who is fully licensed and insured in the State of Oklahoma. As a member of the American Polygraph Association, he upholds the highest standards of professional conduct and testing integrity. With extensive training and a strong foundation in criminal investigations, he delivers dependable and objective polygraph examinations tailored to a wide range of investigative and legal needs.

With a law enforcement career that began in 2007, he brings nearly two decades of experience to the field, including over a decade as a Criminal Investigator since 2013. He is a graduate of the Oklahoma Criminal Investigations Academy (CIA-71), class of 2019, and has conducted extensive investigative work across a range of complex criminal cases.

His formal education includes a Bachelor of Arts in Criminal Justice from the University of Central Oklahoma (2006) and an Associate Degree in Police Science from Oklahoma State University – Oklahoma City (2004). He completed his polygraph training at the Northeast Counterdrug Training Center in Annville, Pennsylvania, where he was trained in validated and scientifically recognized polygraph techniques.

Utilizing the most up-to-date, industry-approved equipment and methodologies, the examiner conducts every examination with precision, professionalism, and adherence to established standards. Examinations are specifically tailored to meet each client’s unique needs, ranging from civil domestic matters to criminal investigations. The examiner’s experience and training ensure that every assessment yields accurate, reliable, and actionable results.
